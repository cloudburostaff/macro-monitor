  • Inflation pipeline (ISM Prices Paid → PPI → CPI → PCE): An Iran / Strait-of-Hormuz energy spike is inflating headline everywhere — CPI +4.2% y/y, PPI +6.5% y/y, euro-area HICP +3.2%, ISM Prices Paid at multi-year highs in both surveys. Five domains corroborate — signal, not noise. But underlying core is easing (core CPI +0.2% m/m, core PPI +0.4% m/m) → the heat is energy, not broad demand.

  • Growth leg (New Orders / Retail / Durables → GDP): A soft Q1 (GDP +1.6%) giving way to a modest Q2 re-firming — ISM New Orders strong in both, IP +0.7%, Retail control +0.5%. Caveats: core capex −1.1% and ISM-employment softness.
